The Story

Salt Lake County has 1/3 of Utah's population and leans Democratic. In 2021, the Legislature split it into all 4 congressional districts, diluting Democratic votes so Republicans won all 4 seats.

In November 2025, a court ordered a fair map that keeps most of Salt Lake County together.
